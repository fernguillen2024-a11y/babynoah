How to Switch to Verizon

Keep your number. Takes less than 10 minutes. Select your current carrier below.

Before You Visit
1

Get Your T-Mobile Transfer PIN

  • Open the T-Life app on your phone
  • Tap Profile → Permissions & Controls
  • Tap Transfer PIN and generate your PIN
  • Or call T-Mobile at 1-800-666-6161 and ask for your Transfer PIN
2

Find Your T-Mobile Account Number

  • In the T-Life app: Account → Account Info
  • Or check your monthly bill — it's printed at the top
  • It's a 9-digit number, not your phone number
3

Do NOT Cancel T-Mobile Yet

Keep your T-Mobile account active. Verizon handles the cancellation automatically once your number transfers.

At Verizon (In-Store or Online)
4

Choose Your Plan & Free Phone

Ask about the 4 lines for $100 Unlimited Welcome deal. You can also bring your existing phone if it's unlocked.

5

Provide Your Info to Verizon

  • Your T-Mobile phone number
  • Your T-Mobile account number
  • Your T-Mobile Transfer PIN

Transfer is usually instant to a few hours.

⚠️

T-Mobile May Push Back

T-Mobile reps are trained to retain you. You are not required to explain — just say "I need my Transfer PIN, please." That's it.

Before You Visit
1

Get Your AT&T Transfer PIN

  • Easiest way: Dial *PORT from your AT&T phone — you'll receive a PIN via text
  • Or log in to att.com → My Account → Port Out / Transfer
  • Or call AT&T at 1-800-331-0500 and request a Number Transfer PIN
2

Find Your AT&T Account Number

  • Log in to att.com → Profile → Account Info
  • Or find it on your paper/PDF bill (top section)
  • AT&T account numbers are typically 9–12 digits
3

Do NOT Cancel AT&T Yet

Leave your AT&T account open. Once the port completes, AT&T will bill you only through your final day of service.

4

Choose Your Plan & Free Phone

5

Provide Your Info to Verizon

  • Your AT&T account number
  • Your AT&T Transfer PIN

AT&T May Offer Retention Deals

AT&T agents may offer discounts to keep you. If you've already decided — just ask for your Transfer PIN and stay focused.

Before You Visit
1

Get Your Transfer / Number Port PIN

  • Cricket: Log in to mycricket.com → My Account → Transfer PIN
  • Boost / Dish: Call 1-888-502-6633 or log in to your account
  • Metro by T-Mobile: Call 1-888-863-8768 and ask for your Transfer PIN
  • US Mobile / Mint / Visible: Log in to your account online → Port Out / Transfer section
  • Any other carrier: Call their customer service and say: "I need my Number Transfer PIN so I can port my number to another carrier."
2

Find Your Account Number

Check your monthly bill or log in to your carrier's app/website → Account Info. It's usually 9–12 digits and not your phone number.

3

Do NOT Cancel Your Plan Yet

Keep your current plan active until your number has fully transferred to Verizon. Canceling early can result in losing your number permanently.

  • Your Transfer / Port-Out PIN

Verizon handles everything. Transfer usually takes minutes to a few hours.

Watch Out for Pushback
